Our Mission

At Paws & Hearts, we believe that no animal should be left behind. Founded in 2026, our mission is to provide a safe haven for abused, abandoned, and neglected dogs. We strive to heal their physical and emotional wounds and match them with forever families where they can thrive.

Our Programs

Rescue & Rehab

Our emergency response team works around the clock to rescue dogs from dangerous situations and provide veterinary care.

Foster Network

We rely on our dedicated network of foster heroes who open their homes to dogs, giving them a warm environment to heal.

Adoption Services

Our thorough matching process ensures that our rescue dogs find the perfect forever families, creating lifelong bonds.
